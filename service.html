Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cleaning, technical, plumbing, and electrical services.

How do I book a service with Urban Clap?

+

You can book a service through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer support. Simply select the required service, choose a convenient time slot, and confirm your booking. Our professional will arrive at your doorstep at the scheduled time.

Are your technicians certified and insured?

+

Yes, all our technicians are fully certified, vetted, and insured. They undergo extensive background checks and professional training before joining our team. We ensure they have the expertise to handle all your electrical, plumbing, and technical requirements safely and effectively.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash. The payment can be made after the service is completed to your satisfaction. For larger projects, we may require a partial advance payment.

Do you provide emergency services?

+

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for electrical and plumbing issues. Our emergency team is available round the clock to address urgent problems like electrical outages, water leaks, or appliance breakdowns that require immediate attention.

What services do you offer for commercial properties?

+

For commercial properties, we offer a wide range of services including electrical system maintenance, plumbing solutions, HVAC servicing, deep cleaning, pest control, and maintenance contracts. We can customize service packages based on your business requirements.

Do you provide warranties on your services?

+

Yes, all our services come with a satisfaction guarantee. We provide a 30-day warranty on labor and workmanship. For parts and materials, the warranty period varies according to the manufacturer's specifications. If you face any issues with our service, we'll fix it at no additional cost.
